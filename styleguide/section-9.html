9.2.kss-section.kss-depth-2

9.2Horizontally gridded

Horizontally ordering is easier, just add the column class to each LI element. Drawbacks to this method include the fact that if the contents of a LI element break to a second line, the it will align with the left of the bullet instead of the left of the top line of text.
